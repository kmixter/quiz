Quiz App

Instructions

To use the quiz app, you need a question and answer file in the following format: 

Q: What is the capital of France?
A: Paris
Q: What is the formula for water?
A: H2O
  1. Click the "Download Example File" button below to download a sample file.
  2. Use a text editor (available on your Chromebook) to edit the file and add your questions.
  3. Click the "Choose File" button below to upload your quiz file and start the quiz!
